June-Bug Punch

Shake up your summer routine with a blend of orange juice, sherbet, ginger ale, and grenadine. Simple to make and even simpler to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups ginger ale
  • 3 scoops orange sherbet
  • ¼ cup grenadine syrup
  • ¼ cup orange juice

Directions

  1. Stir ginger ale, sherbet, grenadine, and orange juice together in a pitcher or punch bowl until smooth.
